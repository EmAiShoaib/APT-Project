Natalie Imbruglia: The Collection is a bootleg and also an unofficial Natalie Imbruglia greatest hits album. It comprises all her released singles, since the first smash-hit Torn to the later releases, including some radio singles that were especially promoted in different parts of the world, the duet with Tom Jones (Never Tear Us Apart), plus the theme song from the Stigmata soundtrack. The bootleg was released by fans in January 2007, experiencing some success between the fans on internet.

Hey Everybody is the Brazilian edition "best of" album by Jennifer Love Hewitt, released in late 2007.

The album features songs from Hewitt's albums Let's Go Bang (1995), Jennifer Love Hewitt (1996) and BareNaked (2002). It also comprises the theme songs from the films I Still Know What You Did Last Summer and The Hunchback of Notre Dame II. The compilation also features an acoustic version of Hewitt's biggest hit, "Can I Go Now".
